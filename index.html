how it's made

stitched to last

Every bouquet is built by hand in our Lahore studio, through the same four steps — slowly, deliberately, the way good things are meant to be made.

01

the yarn

Four-ply combed milk cotton, chosen for colour that holds and fibres that won't fray.

02

the stitch

Every petal and stem is hand-crocheted with even tension, wired to bend without breaking.

03

the wrap

Stems are bundled to your palette, wrapped in kraft paper and mesh, finished with a satin tie.

04

the journey

Boxed to stay still in transit, so it arrives exactly as it left the studio — in full bloom.
